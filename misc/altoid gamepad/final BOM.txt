**Phase 1 - Core Electronics ($200)**

- Get basic functionality working first

**Phase 2 - PCBs & Mechanical ($100)**

- Professional assembly components

**Phase 3 - Polish & Finishing ($64)**
- 3D printing materials, premium consumables
- Final aesthetic touches
